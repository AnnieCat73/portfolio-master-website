02 My story

I am based in London, England, with my daughter and 3 cats! Before I entered the world of tech I did a variety of different roles including running my own business and gaining a degree in Politics and Development Studies.

Through Running my own cat sitting business I gained the ability to efficiently allocate my time and efforts to manage the different demands I faced ahead of me every day. I feel that these skills and being highly self-motivated and upbeat translates well into my coding journey! Other roles in my past, such as being a receptionist, editor, market researcher and volunteer work taught me how to collaborate with others and be part of a team!

When I am not at my computer I enjoy spending time with my 3 Siamese cats and learning and occasionally trading the financial markets!

In the future I love to continue to learn, improve and create beautiful websites!
