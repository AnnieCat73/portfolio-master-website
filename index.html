02 My story

I live in London, England, with my daughter and three cats! Before I entered the world of tech I did a variety of different roles some of which include running my own business and gaining a degree in Politics and Development Studies.

While running my cat-sitting business, I gained the ability to efficiently allocate my time and manage the different demands I faced ahead of me every day. I feel that these skills and being highly self-motivated and upbeat translates well into my coding journey! Other roles in my past, such as being a receptionist, editor, market researcher and a volunteer taught me how to collaborate with others and to be part of a team!

I am now passionate about coding and I enjoy seeing the results of my skills coming into fruition.

When I am not at my computer, I enjoy spending time with my Siamese cats and learning and occasionally trading the financial markets!
